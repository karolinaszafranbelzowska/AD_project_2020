4.1.4 Capitals and Official Languages of North America


Give the SQL command to show for each country in North America:

	- The name of the capital city
	- The name of the country
	- The official language(s)
	- The percentage of people who speak the official language(s) 

The results should be alphabetical city name order, and within that by country name order, 
and within that by language order, and within that by ascending percentage.


Tables: 
	c - city
	co - country
	has - hasvisitedcity
	p - person
	cl - countrylanguage

=======================================================================================================

   mysql> SELECT c.name as name, co.name as name, cl.Language as language, cl.Percentage as percentage
	> FROM city c
	> INNER JOIN country co
	> ON c.ID = co.Capital
	> INNER JOIN countrylanguage cl
	> ON co.Code = cl.CountryCode
	> WHERE cl.IsOfficial = "T"
	> AND co.Continent = "North America"
	> ORDER BY c.Name ASC, co.Name ASC, cl.Language ASC, cl.Percentage ASC;
